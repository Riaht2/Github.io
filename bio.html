    My name is Moriah Hartman, I am 22 years old and I have completed 2 years of education at Eastern Washtington University. I plan to continue my edcuation to finish out my Bachelor's Degree by Winter Quarter, 2020. My degree will be in Graphic Design, more specifically a degree in VCD with a certificate in UX design. With this degree, I plan to find a career that lets me explore my creativity in design and shows off my talent and artistic ability.

    I've always love the aspect of art and have tried any medium I can get my hands on. The one problem I faced growing up was that I wasn't sure what kind of career I could obtain with art. When I first started at Eastern, I was planning to get a degree in Occupational Therapy, unfortunately I couldn't keep up with the science classes, which led me to switch my major into something I am much more passionate about.
